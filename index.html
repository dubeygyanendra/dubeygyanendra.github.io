Selected Research & Engineering Work

Production Generative Systems — Neuropixel.ai

Diffusion · Generative Vision · Production ML

Problem. High-fidelity identity-preserving synthesis under strict production constraints.

Method. Custom diffusion architectures, dataset design, large-scale controlled experimentation, failure-driven iteration.

Results. 50K+ production images, improved visual fidelity, significant artifact reduction.

Failures & Analysis. Diagnosed identity drift, texture instability, and hand synthesis failure modes; refined architectures accordingly.

Core Techniques. Diffusion models, latent control, LoRA fine-tuning, multi-GPU training.

Systems & Tools. PyTorch, CUDA, distributed training, dataset pipelines.

Real-Time Machine Perception System — Manufapp

Computer Vision · Real-Time Systems · Production Deployment

Problem. Extracting machine utilization from noisy live CCTV streams.

Method. Classical CV pipelines transitioning to learning-based detection, custom RTSP ingestion and temporal modeling.

Results. Deployed across 50+ cameras with real-time analytics and monitoring.

Failures & Analysis. Identified robustness limits of rule-based methods under lighting and motion noise.

Core Techniques. Object detection, temporal consistency modeling, noise-robust inference.

Systems & Tools. Python, OpenCV, Flask, AWS, MongoDB.
